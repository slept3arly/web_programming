Emergency Helpline Numbers in India

Medical Emergency Helpline Numbers
Description: A medical emergency helpline is a 24x7 service that provides immediate assistance during health emergencies. It connects individuals to trained medical professionals for quick guidance, ambulance support, and coordination with hospitals.
Phone Number 108
Email ID helpdesk@emri.in

Police Emergency Helpline Numbers
Description: Police emergency helpline provides immediate assistance in cases of crime, theft, accidents, violence, and law enforcement emergencies.
Phone Number 100
Email ID police@gov.in

Fire Emergency Helpline Numbers
Description: Fire emergency helpline offers quick response and rescue services during fire accidents and hazardous situations.
